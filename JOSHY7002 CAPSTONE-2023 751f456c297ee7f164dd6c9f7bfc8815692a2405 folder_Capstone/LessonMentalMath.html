4 MENTAL MATH STRATAGIES

What is Mental-Math?

If you did not already know, mental math is doing simple calculations in your head instead of doing it on paper

1: Multiplying (and dividing) by 5!

When multiplying by 5 there is a neat trick you can do to make it easier to calculate in your head.
Divide the number you want by two then multiply it by 10!. On the contrary if you want to divide a number by 5 just do the opposite, divide by 10 and multiply by 2.

Example:

5 x 45 => 45/2 = 22.5 => 22.5 x 10 = 225 = 5 X 45 = 225

2: Multiplying 2 digit numbers by 11.

This is another simple trick that you can do quickly and easily. If you are multiplying a two digit number by 11 just add them together and put the sum in the middle. If the sum of the numbers go past 1 digit just carry the sum over

Example:

11 x 34 => 3 + 4 = 7 ==> 374 ||| 11 x 86 => 8 + 6 = 14 ==> 946

3: Adding and subtracting trick!

This trick is pretty simple, when adding or subtracting try to split the numbers into easier numbers to work with:

For Example: Addition

214 + 134, first split up the 134 to 100, 30, and 4, then add. ==> 214+100=300, 300+30 = 330, and 330 + 4 = 334.

For Example: Subtraction

432 - 222, again spit up the 222 to 200, 20, 2 then subtract. ==> 432 - 200 = 232, 232 - 20 = 210, 210-2 = 208.

4: Multiplying by 2, 4, 8, 16...

If you want to multiply anything by 2, 4, 8, 16, just keep doubling the number until you get it.

Example:

32 x 16 => 32, 64(x2), 128(x4), 256(x8), 512(x16). So 32 x 16 is 512
